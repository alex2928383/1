Legal & Privacy Policy

Legal & Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

1. Data Collection & Anonymity

EagleVoice does not require or collect names, email addresses, Student IDs, or account registrations. When you submit feedback, we collect:

  • The text content you provide
  • Basic technical data (timestamps and system identifiers)
  • Limited technical info processed by our provider (Supabase) for security

2. How Data Is Stored

Submissions are securely stored using Supabase. While reasonable safeguards are used, no digital system can guarantee absolute security.

3. Purpose of Data Use

Feedback is used exclusively for student advocacy, including identifying concerns and sharing aggregated insights with university stakeholders like the PSAC. No data is sold for advertising.

4. Anonymity Notice

EagleVoice is designed for anonymity. However, true anonymity cannot be absolutely guaranteed. Avoid including personal details if anonymity is your priority.

5. Informal Platform Disclaimer

EagleVoice is an independent initiative and not an official UNT reporting system. Submissions do not constitute formal grievances or Title IX reports.

6. User Conduct

Users agree not to submit content that is threatening, harassing, knowingly false, or in violation of university conduct policies.

7. Limitation of Liability

The platform is provided "as is." The developer is not responsible for data loss, service interruptions, or outcomes resulting from the use of this tool.
